Just what does alternative energy mean?

Throughout human history, it’s been energy in various forms that has allowed the human race to make the leap from the Stone Age to the present day comfortable life style. Energy has lead to the advance of some countries over others, and has also been the underlying cause of conflicts between competing countries. Until just a few years ago, energy was pretty much just taken for granted, as inexpensive fossil fuels were abundant and cheap and used almost exclusively to supply our various energy needs. However, now that reality has set in and we are coming to the understanding those fossil fuels are indeed limited in quantity, polluting our environment and ever increasing in cost, sources of energy other than fossil fuels are being embraced and brought ‘on line’ to help provide our ever increasing energy needs.

So, to answer the question of “What does alternative energy mean?”, I guess we could say that alternative energy is energy sources that do not have direct adverse effects on our environment or disposal issues that ‘traditional’ energy sources do such as fossil fuels and nuclear energy. However, even this description could be controversial as well as what forms of ‘alternative energy’ sources are included in the category.
