Mission Statement

Our main objective is to pull our 'planet' in the right direction - toward Sustainable Living - ASAP

close □

Organizational Description

By gathering what we know into one place, we can learn the methods of building with sustainable products; become more self-sufficient by using alternative methods of energy for our homes and vehicles; increase the use of home gardens eating healthier as we do; learn waste management thus sending less trash to the dump, as well as collaborating with, enjoying and respecting the people and environment we live in.

The ASAP website will work toward this goal with an expansive exchange of ideas, information gathering and dissemination. We will provide a listing of businesses offering sustainable products and services and those learning to use these practices as a matter of daily operation. To aid in awareness and understanding of sustainable living practices, we will provide a variety of learning opportunities presented by experts in various pertinent and related fields.

ASAP will organize, promote and put on seminars and other ‘classroom’ type learning experiences for all of us that will promote alternative energy use, sustainable building methods, energy saving renovations and numerous sustainable living practices all on a free, or low fee basis, to the attendees.

Together we can make a difference in our lives, now, and certainly in the lives of our children in the years to come. In short, let’s do what needs to be done to keep our planet clean, thriving and vibrantly alive!
