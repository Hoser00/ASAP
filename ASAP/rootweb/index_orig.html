ASAP ASAP

A Sustainable Approach Program is a grass roots consortium of diverse individuals, businesses and groups that are "like minded" in the want to advance ALTERNATIVE ENERGY SOURCES, TECHNOLOGIES and LIFESTYLES, and to make this information easily available to anyone interested in obtaining it. The ASAP has no, nor will it allow itself to take on any narrow agenda other than the ability and availability to obtain and be educated on the various alternative energy sources, technologies and lifestyles that are known now or are being developed.

With oil, gas, propane and natural gas prices going into the stratosphere, everyone seems to be jumping onto the "green/sustainable" thing. Well, it's about time, but a little late. Okay, maybe it's never too late, but are all of these services and products that are coming to the market all of a sudden now REALLY going to make a difference, or are they just a big hype by people trying to make money off of a difficult situation for most others? That is what the A Sustainable Approach Program is all about - separate the wheat from the chaff - an exchange of ideas and experiences.
