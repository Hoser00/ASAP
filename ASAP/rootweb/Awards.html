THE ASAP
‘GREEN LEAF’
SUSTAINABILITY AWARD

Often in our fast-paced life we overlook some incredible things that are occurring right under our very own noses. One such thing is when a company/business conducts its daily operation with a high standard of ethics, but goes beyond that to include a great regard for the environment and surrounding local community. These company owners take into consideration how the operation of their businesses affect the world they live in. They understand that there is more to business than just making a profit. Many companies just can’t comprehend this line of thinking, as making the biggest profit possible – at any cost – is the usual method embraced.

The ASAP management wants to recognize and “Spotlight” these community gems that might not get the recognition and acknowledgement they deserve by directing attention to them on our website. Our goal is to make people aware of their accomplishments and in turn patronize these businesses.

Please click on the button to the left to learn about ASAP’s current “Green Leaf” sustainability award winner.
